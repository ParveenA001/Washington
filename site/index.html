What I Know About Architecture:

Part 2 of assignment

Computer architecture is like the building blocks that connect everything together. It brings software and hardware together in a magical way. If I were to explain it in its simplest form, its how we connect all the lego pieces together. If we also look at kind of the stack of how everything works, computer architecture is sat right in the middle of everything. You want to make sure that you can perform specific applications on a device? You need the right architecture.

I think that architecture will continue blooming for generations to come. Its not a field that will fall off, no matter what is thrown its way, that is because we always need stronger and more powerful computers. I see the field growing in a sense where we can create faster, smarter, smaller, and AI based computer everywhere. Truly giving Architects that shine that no one else can take from us. Computers will become stronger and more enrgy efficient and we as architects will always have a place in the workforce.
